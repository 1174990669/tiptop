TipTop

TipTop is located in Shenzhen, the “Capital of Design” and specializes in R&D, design, technical consulting, manufacturing and sales of carbon fiber composite products. With strong technical strength and 14 years of experience in manufacturing, Xinbo is at the leading level in the research and development of high-end composite products.

As a technology-based enterprise, TipTop manufactures high-end carbon fiber, kevlar fiber and glass fiber products with roll-wrapping, pultrusion, winding and prepreg molding technique.

While customizing innovative solutions for customers, TipTop insists on developing and launching new products to adapt to the ever-changing markets demands. With good mechanical properties and chemical stability, TipTop’s products are widely used in high-end industrial equipment, medical equipment, intelligent robots, security equipment, traffic, tools, marine antenna, sporting goods, photography facilities, etc.

Currently, Delong computer is already exported to America, France, Australia, Italy and 80 other countries and regions. Our company has always focused on research, development and innovation . In 2006, we attained ISO 9001 environmentally management certification.And all products certified by CE,FCC,and Rohs

Delong will initiate the second phase of our development strategy. Our company regards “Satisfactory design,reasonable prices, efficient production time and good after-sales service” as our target. We hope to cooperate with more customers for mutual development and benefits. We welcome potential buyers to contact us.
